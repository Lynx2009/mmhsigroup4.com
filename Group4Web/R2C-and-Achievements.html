Response to Covid-19

   When Novel Corona Virus struck the whole world, it is no wonder our lives were greatly affected especially our education. A lot of us had to stay at home to stay safe, our communication with each other also got affected. But despite all of these, Merryland made sure to still give out their best. 
   
   August 10, 2020 - In response to the pandemic, with the given choices of Modular and Online Classes, Merryland chose online class. Merryland believes that this was the best choice to communicate with each other and make it feel like we are still learning while at home. In an online conference, Merryland introduced Schoology – a learning management system that students will use in this new learning set-up. Despite being in Online Class, Merryland proved that each student is still able to strive. Being able to win and join competitions between the barrier of each one of us.
   
   June 9, 2022 - By the time the government and Department of Education (DepEd) is slowly loosening the protocol for the new school year, Merryland had announced the new learning set up to be used in school year 2022-2023. They have decided Hyflex and Pure online. It was said that students are free to choose what learning setup they would like for the new school year.  

   Merryland gave information about these, and it was said that those who chose Pure online will be using zoom and schoology for the whole weekdays for school, while those in hyflex will either have two or three days of face-to-face classes and the rest are online class. It was said that face-to-face classes are mainly for taking quizzes and examinations, for those who are in pure online are required to have a dual camera or mirror when taking quizzes and examination to avoid cheating.

Achievements

   Achievements

        Being able to obtain achievements left and right, as Merryland always made sure to bring out the best of their students, it is no wonder that students not only excel in classrooms but also both school and international competitions. Being able to be a champion or be one of the students in the high ranked lists whenever there are competitions. Merryland trains students by following a certain schedule, they give the best lecture to the competitor and make them understand every single detail of it. Students have now the chance to prove their talents in extra-curricular events.

       In school competitions like National Science Quest that includes Sci-Quiz bee, Sci-Folk dance, Sci-Choric Speech, etc. and other categories too such as MTAP, DSPC (Division Schools Press Conference) and RSPC (Regional Schools Press Conference), PISAY, and more, wherein Merryland had proven its greatness, managing to win CHAMPION countless times. Not only did Merryland prove its greatness in school competitions, but also in international competitions that includes HKIMO (Hong Kong International Mathematical Olympiads), HKISO (Hongkong International Science Olympiad), Hippo International English Language Olympiad World Finals, etc., and as still is, students are able to obtain a high ranked award. That let alone proves the dedication and hard work of both students and teachers in Merryland.

       To add on, Merryland had also been awarded of “One of the Most Outstanding School Awardee”. There are countless students that once studied in Merryland that attained Latin Honors in their perspective universities.
