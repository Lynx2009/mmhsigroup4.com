Casa Department

     Consists of Junior Casa, Senior Casa, and Advanced Casa. For ages 2 ½ - 5 years old. This community offers the start of your children’s path to learning! Starting to basic reading, math, science and other subjects

Elementary Department

     ​Starting from Grade 1 to Grade 6, this community continues your children’s path to learning. Making sure to support students’ needs to learn more. From this, expect more chances of your children’s mind to fully expand. Don’t fret! Merryland teachers are sure to fully guide your kids to a great path.

High School Department:
Junior High School

     Starting from Grade 7 to Grade 10, this community offers a deeper knowledge in different subjects. Performance, written works, competitions, etc. Students will not only gain deeper knowledge but also learn a lot of new different things, not just the basics. These community greatly offers more opportunities of students’ talents!

High School Department:
Senior High School

Starting from Grade 11 to Grade 12, this community now offers academic tracks of STEM, ABM, HUMSS and GAS. These strands help students decide on what career they would want to pursue in the near future.

SPED Curriculum 

For children with learning disabilities, don’t be discouraged just yet! Merryland makes sure that everyone can learn despite the barriers holding you back. Merryland offers special education to students with Autism/ADHD, Down Syndrome and Learning difficulties.
